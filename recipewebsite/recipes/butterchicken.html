BUTTER CHICKEN

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Plain yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chili
  • Canned tomato sauce
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, paprika)
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cut chicken into small pieces and marinate with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice for 30 minutes to an hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© chopped onions until soft and translucent.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and green chili to the pan and cook for another minute.
  4. Add canned tomato sauce, spices, and salt to the pan and simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Blend the sauce until smooth using an immersion blender or by transferring to a blender.
  6. Heat butter in a separate pan and cook the marinated chicken until golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Add the blended sauce to the chicken and simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  8. Stir in heavy cream and simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
  10. Enjoy your homemade butter chicken!
