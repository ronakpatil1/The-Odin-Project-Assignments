Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pizza crust
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings (meat, vegetables, herbs, etc.)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified by your pizza crust recipe or package instructions.
  2. Prepare your pizza crust according to the recipe or package instructions.
  3. Spread pizza sauce over the crust, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Add your desired toppings, such as cheese, meats, vegetables, and herbs.
  5. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for the time specified by your recipe or package instructions, usually around 10-15 minutes.
  6. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  7. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
